Hi, I'm Changrui! Some chase the unknown in the cosmos, my dream is to chase the intricate enigmas nestled within the realm of cryptography and computation. Straddling the edge of what can be believed, known, proved, and solved, I'd like to navigate the intricate boundaries between the computable and the unbounded — the limitations set by the very fabric of our existence.

I am fortunate to be supervised by Professor. Prashant Nalini Vasudevanin my research on cryptography and complexity theory, specifically focusing on Interactive Proofs, for over the past two years. 1

In the summer of 2023, I had the privilege of working closely with Professor Ron D. Rothblumat Technion, focusing on Statistical Zero Knowledge Proofs. 2

In addition to my research endeavors, I have a passion for teaching and disseminating knowledge. I serve as the lead Teaching Assistant for CS3235 Computer Security under the guidance of Professor Reza Shokri and for CS4236 Cryptography Theory and Practice instructed by Professor Prashant Nalini Vasudevan.
